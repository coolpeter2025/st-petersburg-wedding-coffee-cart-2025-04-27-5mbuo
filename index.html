Our Story

Founded in 2020 by passionate coffee artisans, Delightful Bean began with a simple mission: to bring specialty coffee experiences to St. Petersburg's most cherished celebrations. What started as a single mobile cart has grown into the area's premier wedding coffee service, serving hundreds of happy couples and their guests.

Our core values of excellence, creativity, and personalized service drive everything we do. We believe that every wedding deserves not just great coffee, but an unforgettable experience that reflects the unique style and personality of each couple we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your wedding package?

Our comprehensive package includes 3 hours of service, custom menu design, professional baristas, all supplies and equipment, setup and teardown, and unlimited drinks for your guests. Additional hours can be added as needed.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 6-8 months in advance for peak wedding season (March-November). For off-season events, 3-4 months notice is usually sufficient.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer various milk alternatives (oat, almond, soy) and can create custom drinks to accommodate any dietary needs or preferences.

What size spaces can you work with?

Our mobile cart is designed to fit in most venues and requires only a 6'x8' space plus basic power access. We can adapt our setup to both indoor and outdoor locations.

Do you offer tastings before booking?

Yes! We offer complimentary tastings for confirmed bookings, where you can sample our drinks and discuss customization options for your special day.
